Choclate bar hot

Description

Preparation:5 mins
Cook:5 mins
Total:10 mins
Servings:1

Ingredients

  • 1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy bar, chopped
  • â…” cup milk, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon (Optional)

Steps

  1. Place chocolate pieces in a saucepan over medium-low heat; add milk and whisk constantly until chocolate is melted and well blended, about 5 minutes.
  2. Whisk in cinnamon. Remove from heat; add more milk if desired. Serve in a mug.
